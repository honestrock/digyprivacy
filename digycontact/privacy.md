PRIVACY POLICY – DIGYCONTACT
Last updated: 18.02.2026
DigyContact is a guest communication automation platform used by hospitality businesses to respond to guest inquiries through WhatsApp via Meta’s Cloud API.
This Privacy Policy explains how DigyContact processes data when businesses connect their WhatsApp Business Account to our system.

1. Role of DigyContact
DigyContact acts solely as a data processor on behalf of the connected business (for example, a hotel).
We do not independently determine the purpose or means of processing personal data transmitted through WhatsApp conversations.
The connected business remains the data controller.

2. Data We Process
When a guest sends a message via WhatsApp, DigyContact may temporarily process the following information:
Message content
WhatsApp phone number
Conversation metadata such as timestamp and language
This processing is strictly necessary to:
Receive the message
Generate an automated response
Deliver the response back to the guest

3. AI-Based Processing
Messages may be processed through an AI language model in order to generate a contextual response regarding:
Hotel services
Facilities
Guest requests
General informational inquiries
AI processing is performed solely for real-time response generation.
DigyContact does not perform automated profiling or decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

4. Data Retention
DigyContact follows an ephemeral processing architecture.
Messages are:
Processed in real time
Stored only temporarily in system memory
Deleted after successful response delivery
DigyContact does not retain conversation history after processing.
We do not build behavioral profiles of guests.

5. Data Sharing
DigyContact does not sell, share, or use WhatsApp conversation data for:
Marketing
Advertising
Analytics
AI model training
Data is processed only for the purpose of enabling messaging functionality.

6. Third-Party Infrastructure
To operate the service, DigyContact relies on:
Meta Cloud API for message transport
Secure cloud infrastructure providers for hosting and processing
These providers act only as technical infrastructure processors.

7. Security
DigyContact applies industry-standard safeguards including:
Encrypted data transmission
Access controls
Secure processing environments

8. Data Subject Rights
Guests wishing to exercise privacy rights should contact the business they are communicating with.
As DigyContact acts only as a processor, requests are handled through the connected business.
